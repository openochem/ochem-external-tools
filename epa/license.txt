EPA T.E.S.T. descriptors adapted for use inside of OCHEM
 
Provided by Todd Martin, Ph.D., U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
 
They are based on CDK (LGPL 2.0) and use the same license
 
The descriptors were modified to be used inside of OCHEM by BIGCHEM GmbH under the same agreement.
 
The equations used in the descriptor calculations are available in the Molecular Descriptors Guide provided in the help menu of the T.E.S.T. software (https://www.epa.gov/chemical-research/toxicity-estimation-software-tool-test).
